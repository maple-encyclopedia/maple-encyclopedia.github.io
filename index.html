Introduction to Maple Encyclopedia

What is Maple Encyclopedia?

Maple Encyclopedia is a library of information on MapleStory

Why was Maple Encyclopedia created?

Maple Encyclopedia was created to assist new players, returning players, and current players in their progression on the GMS Reboot server.

Where was the information gathered from?

All information on Maple Encyclopedia was gathered from various Reddit threads, YouTube videos, and Discord servers.

What are the differences between the areas?

Early Game - New or Returning Players (Chaos Root Abyss / Hard Magnus)

Mid Game - Players in the mid-game (Damien, Lotus, Lucid)

Master Area - Players in the end-game (Hard Damien, Hard Lotus, Hard Lucid, Hard Will, Black Mage)
